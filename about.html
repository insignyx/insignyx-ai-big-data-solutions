Our Story

  1. 2018: Insignyx Founded

    Began as a small team of data engineers and AI scientists with a vision to make advanced analytics accessible to all businesses. Our founders recognized the growing need for AI-driven solutions in traditional industries and set out to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical business applications.

  2. 2020: Rapid Expansion

    Grew our team to 25+ specialists and launched enterprise projects in healthcare, finance, and retail. During this period, we established partnerships with major cloud providers and developed our proprietary AI frameworks that would become the foundation for our future success.

  3. 2022: Global Recognition

    Achieved significant milestones including Fortune 500 client acquisitions, international expansion, and recognition as one of India's top AI startups. Our innovative solutions in predictive analytics and machine learning automation gained industry acclaim.

  4. 2023+: Leading Innovation

    Pioneering generative AI, large language models, and next-generation data platforms for global clients. We've expanded our team to 50+ experts and continue to push the boundaries of what's possible with artificial intelligence, focusing on ethical AI development and sustainable business transformation.
